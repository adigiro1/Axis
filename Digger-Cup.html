    Countdown to Event

    High Level Event Information

    • When: May 2nd & 3rd 2026
    • What: 2‑day handicap Ryder Cup event in St. Louis
    • Day 1: Four‑Ball
    • Day 2: Singles
    • Scoring:
      • 1 point per match
      • Ties halve the point
      • Most overall points wins
      • Tie Breaker: Total amount of holes won
      • Example:
        • Team 1 combined was 6 up
        • Team 2 combined was 9 up → Team 2 wins
    • Amount of players
      • Want around 16, but can be adjusted. We had 24 players in 2025
      • Need even amount
      • You cannot back out once you have committed
    • Where: Annbriar (Blue Tees)
    • Payout:
      • $100 per winning player
      • 16‑player example: ($50 × 16) / 8 = $100
    • You need to have a handicap to play. If you do not have one, please reach out for details on how to obtain one.
